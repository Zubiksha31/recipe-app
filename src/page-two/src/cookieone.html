Hazelnut and Cocoa Oat Cookies

"Do you often feel hungry between mealtimes? We sure do! These hazelnut and cocoa oat cookies are phenomenal for these times. You'll see soon enough!"

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. 2. Pulverize the rolled oats (you can also just use oat flour).
  3. 3. Add the hazelnut cream, deseeded dates, cocoa, and melted coconut oil to a blender or food processor. Blend until everything is well combined.
  4. 4. Cover a baking sheet with parchment paper. Scoop up some batter with a spoon and form a ball with your hands. Place the cookie ball on the parchment paper.
  5. 5. You can decorate the cookies with some chocolate on top.
  6. 6. Bake for 10 minutes
