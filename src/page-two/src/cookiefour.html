Kiwi, Tangerine and Orange Smoothie

"Do you often feel hungry between mealtimes? We sure do! These Kiwi, Tangerine and Orange Smoothie are phenomenal for these times. You'll see soon enough!"

  1. 1. Cut the oranges and squeeze them in your juicer until you fill a cup of orange juice.
  2. 2. Peel the tangerine and kiwi.
  3. 3. Pour the orange juice, peeled tangerine and kiwi pieces into your food processo
  4. 4. Also chop a couple of fresh cilantro leaves and add them.
  5. 5. Sweeten with a splash of honey or the sweetener you usually use and beat until the ingredients are completely integrated and there are no lumps.
